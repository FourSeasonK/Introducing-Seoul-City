Happy Seoul

Vibrant city                 Brilliant city                 Fantastic city

Seoul is the capital of Korea, the major city of politics, economic and culture.
Although it had a hard time after the Korean War (1950),
it achieved remarkable growth with the hosting of the Seoul Asian Games (1986) and the Seoul Olympics (1988).
It aims to be a vibrant city, full of humility where tradition and modern coexist.
It is full of various arts, cultures, and fantastic entertainment.
